About

about Cheeze Academy

We are a professional school for training skillful cheese makers.

We want everyone to know how tasty the real cheese is.
Cheese used to be always in the meal. Where did it go?

Our mission is changing people's mind through cheese.

We are going to support almuni for their cheesey career.
We also provide almuni with the full support for job hunting.

Course

Curriculam

Our curriculam is based on many professionals' advices. Wanna learn the deep side of cheese? Don't miss this chance!

Intership at professionals' field

Students can have a choice for doing internship at professionals' field and factories.They learn everything about cheese through doing their work. We believe that all the students will be able to make a brilliant cheese through the experiences.

Acquire the knowledge of the real cheese

Needless to say, skills won't be effective if they don't have the knowledge. At our academy, professional teachers give the full support and lectures not only to know about cheese but also to learn the essential knowledge like nutrition.

Capstone with the tasting audition.

During the last 2 months, students will work on their own capstone. They will make the actual cheese and have the audition at which guests will taste their cheese. Only one tip for getting their attention; "Get inspired by cheese and inspire the cheese".
